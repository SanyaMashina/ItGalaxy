Integration

ItGalaxy company offers a full range of IT integration services - consultations, preparation of technical specifications, system design and development of solutions that will allow all employees to work in a "one—stop shop" mode.

Modern websites, depending on their direction and type, can include from one to several million pages. To independently change the information and content of the project, the owner is provided with a resource management system on the Network. But this is usually not enough, especially for customers with large websites or online stores, because they can contain more than one thousand products. Each of them has its own cost, description and balance. If you use only the management system, then the owner will have to do double work: edit data in the accounting program and on the platform itself. The optimal solution for such a task will be the integration of the site with external services.
